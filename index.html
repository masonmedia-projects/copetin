Copetín
Restaurant & Bar

Copetin Restaurant and Bar serves Claudio Aprile's inventive, elevated Canadian cuisine informed by the diverse food personalities of Toronto's ethnic neighbourhoods. Located at corner of King and Church in Toronto, Copetin features a stylish dining room, relaxed bar and lively patio. Chef Aprile is one Canada's most celebrated, visionary chefs and judge of CTV's MasterChef Canada.
